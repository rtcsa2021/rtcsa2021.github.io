Registration

General Registration Information for AUTHORS inside Mainland China

  • At least one author of each accepted paper must register the conference to ensure that the paper will be included in the conference proceedings. The author registration fee is RMB ¥3500 for IEEE member and RMB ¥4300 for non-IEEE member, respectively, plus bank service charge. Note that each accepted paper must be registered before June 17, 2014, and there is NO late registration for authors..

  • Payment of a registration fee covers the cost to attend all conference activities （IEEE RTCSA 2014， IEEE NVMSA 2014 and IWMSA 2014）, coffee breaks, conference reception and banquet, and all lunches and dinners during the conference. In addition, each registrant will receive a copy of the conference proceedings in a USB drive.

  • Each paper (Full paper or Short paper) needs an UNIQUE author registration. Multiple papers with same authors require multiple author registrations. 

  • The authors can follow the steps below to finish the registration.

    After Registration:

  • The registration chair will send a confirmation email to you when your registration fee is successfully received.

  • You can contact the registration chair, Prof. Yong Feng at rtcsa2014@gmail.com regarding invitation letters of conference participation after you have registered. We can only provide such letters once your registration fee has been paid. The letters will be provided to you by the local arrangements chair from China. Invitation letters will be issued only to the authors outside China.

  • Receipt/invoice of the registration fee and the USB drive containing the conference proceedings will be available at the conference venue. We will not send you receipt/invoice/conference proceedings through postal service after the conference.
