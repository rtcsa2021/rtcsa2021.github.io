Registration

General Registration Information for AUTHORS outside Mainland China

  • At least one author of each accepted paper must register the conference to ensure that the paper will be included in the conference proceedings. The author registration fee is USD $550 for IEEE member and USD $680 for non-IEEE member, respectively, plus bank service charge. Note that each accepted paper must be registered on or before June 17, 2014, and there is NO late registration for authors.

  •  Payment of a registration fee covers the cost to attend all conference activities （IEEE RTCSA 2014， IEEE NVMSA 2014 and IWMSA 2014）, coffee breaks, conference reception and banquet, and all lunches and dinners during the conference. In addition, each registrant will receive a copy of the conference proceedings in a USB drive.

  • Each paper (Full paper or Short paper) needs an UNIQUE author registration. Multiple papers with same authors require multiple author registrations.
